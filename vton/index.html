Ongoing Research: Virtual Apparel Experiences Using Diffusion Models

Project Overview:

This ongoing research employs diffusion models to generate photorealistic and highly accurate virtual experiences. It can be used to enhance online shopping for consumers through a personalized experience .

Impact:

  • Small Business Owners: Small business owners can leverage this technology to create dynamic, virtual catalogs without the need for physical inventory for photoshoots.
  • Consumers: Consumers can better visualize how clothing items would look on them, leading to more informed purchasing decisions and reducing return rates.
