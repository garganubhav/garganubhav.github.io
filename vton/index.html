Ongoing Research: Virtual Experiences Using Diffusion Models

Project Overview:

This ongoing research employs a diffusion model to create highly realistic and precise images. This technology aims to improve the online shopping experience by offering consumers a tailored and personalized approach.

Impact:

  • Small Business Owners: They can leverage this technology to create virtual catalogs without the need for physical inventory for photoshoots.
  • Consumers: They can better visualize how clothing items would look on them, leading to more informed purchasing decisions and reduced return rates.
